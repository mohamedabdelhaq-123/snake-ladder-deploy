The Objective

Be the first player to reach Square 100. Roll the dice, move forward, climb ladders to go up faster, and avoid snakes that pull you down.

  • Start from Square 1 (start position).
  • Move exactly the number shown on the dice.
  • If you reach 100, you win.

Quick Start

  1. Choose players (2 to 4) and set names.
  2. Press Start Game to load the board.
  3. On your turn, click Roll Dice.
  4. Your token moves automatically and snakes/ladders apply instantly.
  5. Keep playing until someone reaches Square 100.
